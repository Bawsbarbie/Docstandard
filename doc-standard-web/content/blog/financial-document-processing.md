## The Cost of Structural Inconsistency

Most financial errors stem from "Schema Fragmentation." When one branch extracts a `Billing_ID` and another extracts an `Invoice_Ref`, your global reporting fails. This inconsistency forces high-cost finance teams to spend hours performing manual "Excel Triage" before every month-end close.

DocStandard replaces manual cleanup with deterministic normalization rules. We enforce your master data definitions on every field, ensuring 100% relational integrity across your global supply chain.

### Technical Processing Benchmarks:
* **Relational Field Binding:** Linking every `Tax_Amount` and `Fuel_Surcharge` to its parent reference ID for automated audit trails.
* **Format-Specific Routing:** Delivering data in the exact format required by your endpoint (e.g., CSV for QuickBooks, JSON for proprietary ERPs).
* **Multi-Page Reconciliation:** Maintaining row-level integrity for line items that span across multiple document pages.
