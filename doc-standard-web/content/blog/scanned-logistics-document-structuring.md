## The Problem with "Dirty" Image Data

Scanned documents are inherently messy. Smudges, skewed lines, and low-contrast text cause standard OCR to fail at a rate of 15-20%. In a high-stakes customs audit, a 20% error rate on `Total_Declared_Value` is a legal liability.

DocStandard applies a two-layer structuring protocol. First, we clean the image using advanced pre-processing logic. Second, we apply our logistics-aware extraction engine to map the text to a high-integrity schema.

### The Structuring Protocol:
* **Pre-Processing Cleanup:** Deskewing, denoising, and contrast-normalization to ensure the highest possible text recognition.
* **Schema-Aware Extraction:** Mapping extracted text to specific logistics fields like `Carrier_SCAC`, `Equipment_ID`, and `Port_of_Loading`.
* **Field Integrity Checks:** Automated validation of date formats, weights, and numeric values to catch errors before they hit your system.

## Building a Searchable Document Archive

Once a scanned document is structured, it becomes a searchable asset. You can instantly query your entire history for a specific `Reference_Number` or `SKU`, turning a mountain of paper into a powerful business intelligence tool.

### Operational Benefits:
1. **Accelerated Ingestion:** Move data from paper to ERP in minutes, not days.
2. **Audit Readiness:** High-integrity structured records that satisfy 5-year retention requirements.
3. **Reduced Rework:** Clean, validated data that eliminates the need for manual corrections.

By mastering the structuring of scanned logistics documents, DocStandard helps you reclaim control over your operational data.
