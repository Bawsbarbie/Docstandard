## The Challenge of Historical Data Decay

Legacy records often suffer from "Data Decay." Low-resolution scans from the early 2000s or faded thermal paper receipts make standard OCR ingestion impossible. Without a dedicated pre-processing layer, these records remain unreadable by modern ERP and Business Intelligence (BI) tools.

DocStandard applies a restorative normalization layer to every legacy batch. We clean, deskew, and contrast-enhance historical scans before applying our domain-specific extraction logic.

### Legacy Conversion Standards:
* **Image Restoration Logic:** Enhancing low-DPI scans to increase extraction fidelity for ancient reference numbers and hand-annotated dates.
* **Schema Retrofitting:** Mapping legacy data fields to modern ERP requirements (e.g., converting legacy carrier codes into standard SCAC formats).
* **Batch Temporal Integrity:** Ensuring that 10-year archives maintain their chronological and relational integrity during the digitization process.

## Reclaiming Business Intelligence from the Past

Once legacy records are normalized, they become active assets. You can perform multi-year spend analysis, identify long-term vendor performance trends, and meet the 5-year statutory record-keeping requirements for customs compliance with zero manual effort.

### Strategic Outcomes:

2. **Historical Spend Visibility:** Normalize years of freight bills to identify carrier overcharges and contract business rule failures.
3. **Storage Cost Reduction:** Transition from high-cost physical storage or unsearchable digital silos to a lean, structured data warehouse.
