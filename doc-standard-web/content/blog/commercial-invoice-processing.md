# Commercial Invoice Processing: Mastering Global Trade Data (2026)

The commercial invoice is the primary legal record of a trade transaction, yet it remains one of the most difficult documents to automate. With complex line items, multi-currency values, and varying tax requirements, standard OCR fails to deliver the precision needed for customs entry or financial audit. Commercial invoice processing is the technical protocol for normalizing these high-density records into high-integrity data assets.

DocStandard provides deterministic commercial invoice normalization, ensuring your trade records are 100% compliant and system-ready.

## The Friction of Semantic Data Density

Commercial invoices are dense. A single file can list 500 SKUs, each with its own `Unit_Price`, `Extended_Value`, and `Country_of_Origin`. Generic tools struggle with this density, often mis-mapping fields or losing line-item integrity across page breaks. In a customs audit, a 1% error rate on `Declared_Value` results in legal liability.

DocStandard replaces probabilistic guessing with field-level logic. We normalize every field into a validated schema that satisfies both your broker's ABI requirements and your controller's GL rules.

### Extraction Logic Benchmarks:
* **Line-Item Relational Mapping:** Ensuring that every SKU is mathematically tied to its corresponding `Net_Weight` and `Extended_Value`.
* **Incoterm Validation:** Automatically extracting and validating `FOB`, `CIF`, or `EXW` codes for correct valuation calculation.
* **Master SKU Alignment:** Automatically converting vendor-specific descriptions into your internal master product definitions.
