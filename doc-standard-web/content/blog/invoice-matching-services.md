Mismatches are often structural. A PO might use `SKU-A` while an invoice uses `PART-123`. A generic system flags this as an error, even if the items are identical. This "Semantic Gap" forces your AP team to play "Detective," wasting thousands of hours annually in manual data cross-referencing.

DocStandard replaces "Manual Detection" with "Deterministic Normalization." We map all IDs to your master data definitions during the extraction phase, ensuring that when data hits your ERP, the matching engine sees a perfect alignment.
