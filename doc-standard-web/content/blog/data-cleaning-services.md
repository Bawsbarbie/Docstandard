## The Friction of Unstructured Data Decay

Legacy databases and manual entry systems are prone to data decay. When one user enters a date as `DD/MM/YYYY` and another as `MM-DD-YY`, your automated reporting fails. This inconsistency forces high-cost analysts to spend hours performing manual "Excel triage" instead of driving business value.

DocStandard applies a deterministic cleaning layer. We normalize field formats, resolve naming conflicts, and repair broken relational links across your entire document history.

### Cleaning Layer Benchmarks:
* **Fuzzy Logic Deduplication:** Identifying near-matches in vendor names (e.g., "FedEx Ground" vs "FedEx Grnd") to unify spend reporting.
* **Format Standardization:** Enforcing ISO-standard date and numeric strings across all ingested document batches.
* **Contextual Data Repair:** Using master reference lists to fill in missing fields (like a missing `VAT_ID` based on the `Vendor_Name`).
