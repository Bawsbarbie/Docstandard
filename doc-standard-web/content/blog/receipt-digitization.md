# Receipt Digitization: Transforming Expense Data into Audit Assets (2026)

Operational expenses are often the most difficult data to track. Whether it is warehouse supplies, transport repairs, or regional branch costs, receipts are typically unsearchable, physical liabilities. Receipt digitization is the process of normalizing these fragmented records into structured, high-integrity data assets that drive automated expense reporting and tax reconciliation.

DocStandard provides deterministic receipt normalization, ensuring every expense is captured with 100% precision.

## The Problem with Low-Resolution Expense Data

Thermal paper receipts fade, skew, and smudge. Standard OCR fails to extract the `Vendor_Name` or `Total_Tax` from a grainy image, resulting in "Missing Expenses" and failed VAT reclamation. For a finance director, this is a "Silent Loss" that accumulates across thousands of small transactions.

DocStandard applies a restoration-first protocol. We enhance the raw scan quality before our extraction engine maps the data to your specific GL account strings.

### Receipt Digitization Benchmarks:
* **Pre-Extraction Image Restoration:** Enhancing faded text and removing shadows to increase extraction accuracy by 35%.
* **Automatic Categorization Logic:** Mapping expenses to correct GL codes (e.g., 'Travel', 'Operations', 'Freight') based on vendor master lists.
* **Tax Split Extraction:** Deterministically separating base amounts from VAT/Sales Tax for automated tax reporting.
