Trade compliance is a binary environment: you are either compliant or you are facing a violation. Export licenses, ECCN classifications, and end-user statements represent a critical "Documentation Moat" that must be 100% accurate. Export license normalization is the technical process of extracting and validating these records into structured data assets that drive automated compliance screening.

DocStandard provides deterministic compliance normalization, ensuring your trade records are 100% audit-proof.

## The Friction of Regulatory Data Density

Compliance documents are dense. An export license can contain dozens of restrictions, `Value_Limits`, and `Expiration_Dates`. Standard OCR fails to maintain the "Legal Context" of these fields, leading to "Compliance Drift" where your system unknowingly processes a shipment against an expired license.

DocStandard replaces probabilistic guessing with validation logic. We normalize every field into a high-integrity schema that satisfies the requirements of AES and other global filing systems.

### Compliance Protocol Benchmarks:
* **License Limit Tracking:** Automatically extracting and reconciling `Available_Balance` on licenses to prevent over-shipping violations.
* **Entity Screening Logic:** Deterministically mapping `Consignee` and `End-User` details for automated denied party screening.
* **Audit Retention Records:** Delivering high-fidelity digital assets that meet the mandatory 5-year statutory retention period.
