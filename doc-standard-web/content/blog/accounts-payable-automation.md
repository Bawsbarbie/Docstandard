## The Bottleneck of Manual Intervention

The "Automation Gap" exists when a system correctly extracts data but cannot validate it against your internal ledger rules. If your ERP requires a `Cost_Center` and a `GL_Code` for every line item, but your extraction tool only provides a `Description`, your automation fails. This "Missing Logic" forces your AP team into a cycle of manual data enrichment.

DocStandard solves this by applying your specific business logic during the extraction phase, ensuring data enters your ERP in a "Posting-Ready" state.

### Automation Protocol Benchmarks:
* **Three-Way Match Readiness:** Automatically structuring extracted `PO_Numbers` and `Line_Item_Quantities` for seamless comparison against receipts and orders.
* **Master Ledger Alignment:** Enforcing your specific chart of accounts on every extracted document batch.
* **Auto-Default Rules:** Applying preset GL coding based on vendor category or historical patterns to accelerate the approval workflow.
