## Pain Section

### Platform Selection Challenges

<div class="grid grid-cols-3 gap-8">

**Integration Complexity**
CargoWise's proprietary eAdapter/XML approach requires specialized expertise for ERP integrations. Flexport's modern REST APIs offer easier integration but with less depth for complex forwarding workflows. Both create integration challenges that manual data entry cannot resolve.

**Total Cost Uncertainty**
CargoWise's per-shipment pricing model creates variable costs that scale with volume. Flexport's service-plus-software pricing bundles freight rates with platform access, obscuring true software costs. Both models create budgeting challenges for growing operations.

**Customization vs. Standardization**
CargoWise offers extensive customization that enables complex workflows but creates rigid, difficult-to-maintain configurations. Flexport's standardized workflows limit customization but ensure consistency and easier onboarding. Finding the right balance is critical.

## FAQ Section

### CargoWise vs Flexport FAQs

**Can I use DocStandard with both CargoWise and Flexport?**
Yes. DocStandard supports both platforms with pre-built connectors. Many organizations use CargoWise for their forwarding operations while using Flexport for specific trade lanes or customer relationships. DocStandard normalizes data from both sources into consistent formats for your ERP.

**Which platform has better ERP integration?**
CargoWise offers deeper, more mature integrations with enterprise ERPs but requires more complex configuration through eAdapter/XML. Flexport offers easier REST API integrations but with less depth for complex forwarding workflows. DocStandard bridges both platforms to major ERPs, reducing integration complexity regardless of your choice.

**Is CargoWise or Flexport better for small forwarders?**
Flexport's model targets shippers rather than forwarders—if you're a forwarder, CargoWise is designed for your business model. However, smaller forwarders often struggle with CargoWise's implementation complexity and costs. Consider CargoWise One (the simplified version) or alternatives like Magaya for smaller operations.

**How do the pricing models compare?**
CargoWise typically charges per shipment plus module fees, creating variable costs that scale with volume. Flexport bundles freight rates with platform access, making software costs less visible but potentially higher overall. For high-volume operations, CargoWise's per-shipment model often becomes more economical; for lower volumes, Flexport's bundled approach may be simpler.

**Can I switch from one platform to the other?**
Platform switching is possible but expensive—expect 12-18 months of disruption, data migration challenges, and retraining costs exceeding $1M for mid-size operations. DocStandard can help bridge platforms during transitions by maintaining ERP data flows while you migrate operations.
